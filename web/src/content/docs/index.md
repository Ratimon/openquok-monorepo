## What is it for ?

**Openquok** is a controlled content pipeline that sits on top of your AI tools like **OpenClaw**—turning high-volume generation into high-quality output. It collects everything your agents create, routes it through a structured approval flow, and ensures only the right content gets scheduled and published.

Instead of flooding a single calendar with unchecked posts, Openquok gives you a clear system: generate at scale, review in one place, and stay in control of what actually goes live.


## Overview

Four stages, built for control. The more you run it, the better your content gets.

<CardGrid>
<Card title="Generate" icon="✨">
Openclaw produces platform-ready content at scale — visuals, captions, and post structure
</Card>
<Card title="Review" icon="📋">
Everything flows into a pre-approval queue and lands as private drafts — filter, edit, add final touches like trending sounds, and decide what goes live
</Card>
<Card title="Schedule" icon="📅">
Only approved content is scheduled and organized across your workspaces
</Card>
<Card title="Improve" icon="📊">
Performance feeds back into your pipeline — so next week’s content gets better
</Card>
</CardGrid>
